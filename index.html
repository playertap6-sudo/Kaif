Who I Am

Philosophy of Engineering Student

I'm an undergraduate student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, exploring the philosophy of engineering — examining the ideas, values, and ethical frameworks that shape how engineers think and work. This portfolio is a living document of my academic journey.

Philosophy of Engineering bridges the gap between abstract thought and practical design. From understanding the ethics of technology to analyzing the history of engineering decisions, every topic opens new perspectives on what it means to build and create responsibly.
